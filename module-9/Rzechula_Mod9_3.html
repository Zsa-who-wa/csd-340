The Team I Know

There’s a team I know that’s home to _______.
They’re not all-round champs, but we still give them claps.

Their field is called _______ that carries real ivy
and green grass that their cleats can’t grasp.

They have real bleacher _____ and fans that think they’re number one.
They still work the scoreboard by hand and still make teams mad.

When there seems to be an end they come back again
hitting line drives left and ____ and winning the fight.
